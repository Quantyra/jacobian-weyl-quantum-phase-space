## 1. Axiom packages

### Axiom pack **C1c-C0** (abelian vanishing-at-infinity)

1. \(A_0:=C_0(\mathbb{R}^3)\) with the sup norm.  
2. Polynomials \(\mathbb{C}[q_0,q_1,q_2]\) sit densely in a \(*\)-subalgebra of continuous functions in the sense of restriction to the generators (polynomial functions).  
3. A **unital \(*\)-homomorphic extension** of \(\psi|_{\mathbb{C}[q]}\) is a unital \(*\)-endomorphism \(\overline{\psi}:A_0\to A_0\) with \(\overline{\psi}(f)=f\circ F\) on polynomial functions.

### Axiom pack **C1c-Bogoliubov** (quasifree CCR)

1. \(A_{\mathrm{CCR}}\) is the CCR \(C^*\)-algebra over \((\mathbb{R}^{6},\sigma_{\mathrm{std}})\) generated by Weyl unitaries \(W(z)\).  
2. A **Bogoliubov / quasifree endomorphism** is induced by a linear (or affine) symplectic contraction on the one-particle space: field operators transform linearly, \(\varphi(f)\mapsto\varphi(Tf)\).  
3. Any such endomorphism acts **linearly** on the one-particle coordinates \((q,p)\).

### Axiom pack **C1c-GenCP** (general unital CP)

1. Domain some \(C^*\) completion \(A\supset \mathcal{W}\) of the polynomial Weyl algebra.  
2. Unital CP map \(\Phi:A\to A\) (or into \(B(H)\)) extending \(\psi\) on \(\mathcal{W}\).  
3. **No** Bogoliubov or \(*\)-homomorphism assumption.

**Lemma C1c.1.**

## 3. Theorem — NO-GO for C1c-C0

**Theorem C1c-A (C0 \(*\)-extension blocked).**  
There is **no** unital \(*\)-endomorphism \(\overline{\psi}:C_0(\mathbb{R}^3)\to C_0(\mathbb{R}^3)\) extending \(f\mapsto f\circ F\) on polynomial functions.

**Proof.**  
If \(F\) is not proper, composition does not preserve \(C_0\): there exist \(q_n\) with \(|q_n|\to\infty\) and \(F(q_n)\to y_\star\in\mathbb{R}^3\). Choose \(0\le f\in C_0(\mathbb{R}^3)\) with \(f(y_\star)=1\). Then \(f(F(q_n))\to 1\not\to 0\), so \(f\circ F\notin C_0(\mathbb{R}^3)\). ∎

**Corollary.**  
No unital \(*\)-homomorphic extension of \(\psi\) to any \(C^*\) algebra in which the \(C_0\) functions of the position generators sit in the standard way and are preserved by the extension can restrict to a \(*\)-endo of \(C_0(\mathbb{R}^3)\). In particular, \(\psi\) is not the restriction of a unital \(*\)-endomorphism of \(C_0(\mathbb{R}^3)\).

## 4. Theorem — NO-GO for C1c-Bogoliubov

**Theorem C1c-B (Bogoliubov / linear quasifree blocked).**  
There is **no** Bogoliubov endomorphism of \(A_{\mathrm{CCR}}\) whose restriction to the polynomial Weyl algebra agrees with \(\psi\).

**Proof.**  
Bogoliubov endomorphisms act linearly on one-particle field coordinates. But
\[
\psi(q_i)=F_i(q)
\]
with \(F\) a **nonlinear** polynomial map (degree 3; e.g.\ \(F_0\) contains \((1+xy)^3 z\)). This cannot equal a linear image \(Tq\) on generators. ∎
