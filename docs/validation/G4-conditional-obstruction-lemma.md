# G4 ESS obstruction lemma — final form (A001)

**Supersedes** earlier “conditional only” framing for the *existence* claim.

## Theorem (ESS obstruction, existential)
Let \(P_j^{\mathrm{sym}}=-i X_j\) on \(C_c^\infty(\mathbb{R}^3)\) for A001 dual fields \(X_j\) (div-free).  
**There exists** \(j\in\{0,1,2\}\) such that \(P_j^{\mathrm{sym}}\) is **not essentially self-adjoint** in \(L^2(\mathbb{R}^3)\).
