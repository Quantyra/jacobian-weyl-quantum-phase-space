# G6 CP / dilations / channels — A001

**Status:** CLOSED as **negative structural package** (ladder F)  
**Date:** 2026-07-21  
**Story:** S022

## Setup
G3 gives a **unital algebra endomorphism** \(\psi\) of the polynomial Weyl algebra (multiplicative, preserves \(*\) formally when \(B\) real and div-free).

## Facts
1. Multiplicative \(\psi\) is **not** automatically a Heisenberg-picture quantum channel on \(B(H)\).  
2. Channels require **normal unital CP** maps on a von Neumann algebra (or CP on \(C^*\)).  
3. **Stinespring** dilations apply to CP maps; algebra endomorphisms that extend to automorphisms are implemented by unitaries (if inner); **proper** endomorphisms need correspondence/Cuntz/Hilbert-module pictures (charter WP4).  
4. Classical non-injectivity of \(\Phi\) and non-surjectivity of \(F\) ⇒ \(\psi\) is **not an automorphism** of the polynomial algebra ⇒ cannot be unitary conjugation on a faithful rep of the full algebra.

## Verdict
- **No CP/channel claim authorized.**  
- If a future G4/G5 extension exists and is CP, non-surjectivity suggests **information loss / subsystem embedding**, not a reversible gate.  
- G6 gate **fails** for reversible quantum operations; remains open only for exotic open-system interpretations pending G4–G5.
